Game Rules

1.There will be 3 levels in the game.
2.You will have 10 attempts for level 1, 7 Attempts for level 2, 5 Attempts for level 3.
3.For each right guess you will get 10 points in each level!
4.Try not to waste attempts!The more you guess correct words the more you gain points.
5.It's a fun game give it a try!
